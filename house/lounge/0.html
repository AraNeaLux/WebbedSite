A Lounge

A place of gentle friendships, light conversation, and lighter spirits.

Dazzling flecks of light reflected and refracted in crystal. The delicate balance of conversation, danced through with ease, slightly off-kilter. A whirlwind of trifling topics, small curiosities, quiet questions, always posed but not previously heard. Smiles and sparkling eyes and whispers in your ears and silk between your fingers.
