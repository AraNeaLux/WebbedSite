A Private Chamber

A place of soft seclusion, warm nostalgia, and reassurance.

Piles of fluffy blankets with varying textures that can and will eat you if you lie on them, snow falling gently past a streetlamp at night, the oldish Minecraft music (Do DO do Do DO do do), secret candy stashes that are definitely not over a year old, those slide whistle pops which definitely deserve distinction, fire escapes on clear windy nights, gaudy plastic Mardi Gras beads in memory of a friend.
