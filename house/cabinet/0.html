A Dark Cabinet

A place of things hastily stashed away, small creatures lurking in hidden corners, and doors which conceal disorder.

A lonely highway at night with rain on the roads reflecting the stars. The shadows beneath every bush and tree under the feeble glow of a streetlamp. Quiet nighttime noises which you can't quite tell are real or imaginary. A candle sputtering, burning at both ends, the smoke choking as it spirals into the air. The weight of silence on your chest and in the back of your throat, caught before it reaches your tongue.
